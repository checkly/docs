## Overview

The List Status Pages endpoint returns all status pages configured in your Checkly account. Status pages provide public visibility into your service health and incident communications.

**Common Use Cases**:
- Status Page Management
- Public Communication
- Service Health Display
- Incident Communication

<Note>
Status pages are public-facing communications tools. Use them to maintain transparency with your users about service availability and ongoing incidents.
</Note>
