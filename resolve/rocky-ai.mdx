# Rocky AI

Rocky AI is Checkly's intelligent assistant that helps you resolve issues faster by providing AI-powered insights, automated analysis, and intelligent recommendations.

## What is Rocky AI?

Rocky AI is an intelligent observability assistant that:

- Analyzes your monitoring data to identify patterns and anomalies
- Provides intelligent recommendations for issue resolution
- Automates routine troubleshooting tasks
- Helps you understand complex system behaviors

## Key Capabilities

### Intelligent Alert Analysis
Rocky AI analyzes alerts and incidents to:
- Identify root causes and patterns
- Suggest potential solutions
- Provide context and background information
- Recommend preventive measures

### Automated Troubleshooting
Automate common troubleshooting tasks:
- Run diagnostic checks
- Analyze logs and traces
- Correlate related events
- Generate incident reports

### Performance Optimization
Get AI-powered recommendations for:
- Performance bottlenecks
- Resource optimization
- Configuration improvements
- Scaling strategies

### Predictive Analytics
Anticipate potential issues:
- Identify trends that may lead to problems
- Suggest proactive measures
- Predict resource needs
- Forecast capacity requirements

## Getting Started with Rocky AI

### 1. Enable Rocky AI
Activate Rocky AI in your Checkly dashboard and configure your preferences.

### 2. Connect Data Sources
Connect your monitoring data, logs, and traces to enable comprehensive analysis.

### 3. Configure AI Preferences
Set up your preferences for:
- Analysis depth and frequency
- Notification preferences
- Recommended actions
- Privacy and data handling

### 4. Start Using Insights
Begin receiving AI-powered insights and recommendations for your systems.
