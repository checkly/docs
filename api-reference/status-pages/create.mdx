## Overview

The Create Status Page endpoint allows you to create new public status pages for communicating service health and incidents to your users. Configure branding, services, and notification settings.

**Common Use Cases**:
- Public Status Communication
- Service Health Display
- Incident Management
- Customer Communication

<Note>
Status pages become publicly accessible once created. Configure appropriate branding, messaging, and service groupings to match your organization's communication standards.
</Note>
