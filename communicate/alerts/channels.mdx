Alert channels determine **how** alert notifications reach your team when checks fail, degrade, or recover. Checkly supports a wide range of notification methods to fit your team's communication preferences and operational workflows.

## Available Alert Channels

### Communication Platforms
- [**Email**](/integrations/alerts/email): Direct email notifications
- [**Slack**](/integrations/alerts/slack): Team channel integration  
- [**Discord**](/integrations/alerts/discord): Community and team chat
- [**Telegram**](/integrations/alerts/telegram): Mobile messaging
- [**SMS & Phone**](/integrations/alerts/sms): Text messages and voice calls
- [**Microsoft Teams**](/integrations/alerts/msteams): Enterprise team collaboration

### Incident Management
- [**PagerDuty**](/integrations/incident-management/pagerduty): On-call management
- [**Opsgenie**](/integrations/incident-management/opsgenie): Alert orchestration
- [**FireHydrant**](/integrations/incident-management/firehydrant): Incident response
- [**Incident.io**](/integrations/incident-management/incidentio): Incident coordination
- [**Rootly**](/integrations/incident-management/rootly): Post-incident analysis
- [**ilert**](/integrations/incident-management/ilert): Alert automation
- [**Splunk On-Call**](/integrations/incident-management/splunk_on_call): Enterprise alerting
- [**Spike.sh**](/integrations/alerts/spike): Developer alerts

### Custom Integrations
- [**Webhooks**](/integrations/alerts/webhooks): Custom API endpoints
- [**StatusPage**](/integrations/alerts/statuspage): Public status pages
